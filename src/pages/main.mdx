# GPU Wars: Five Turning Points That Changed Computing

*Welcome to the virtual exhibit exploring the remarkable evolution of the Graphics Processing Unit (GPU) — from a specialized graphics accelerator to one of the most influential computing technologies of the modern era.*

<hr class="trace-divider" />

## Welcome

The **GPU Wars Virtual Exhibit** invites you to explore one of the most significant transformations in computer architecture. What began as dedicated hardware for rendering graphics has evolved into a massively parallel computing platform that powers artificial intelligence, scientific research, high-performance computing, and modern interactive experiences.

This exhibit is organized as a series of galleries. Each section highlights a different stage in the GPU's evolution, allowing you to explore at your own pace — though following the recommended sequence gives the deepest sense of how the technology evolved over time.
